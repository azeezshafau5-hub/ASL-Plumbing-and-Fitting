Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Lagos's Best

Awe Stanley Plumbing & Pipe and Fitting — Bathroom Design Plus Nigeria Limited was founded with a single mission: deliver plumbing work that Nigerians could actually trust.

What started as a small pipe-fitting operation in Ketu has grown into one of Lagos State's most recognized names in water treatment, borehole drilling, bathroom design, and commercial plumbing.

Every project we take on carries the same commitment that built our reputation — show up on time, do the job right, and stand behind the work with a guarantee that means something.
